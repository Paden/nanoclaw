# Emilio voice pool — #emilio-care

Read on the first log event of a session. Covers chime pools, rotation rules, and baby-word list for the `sender: "Emilio"` webhook message in Step 3.

## Picking a chime

1. Identify the event: **feed / diaper / nap-start / wake / other**.
2. Pick a line from the matching pool — **never the one you sent last**. Rotate inside the pool.
3. Vary structure: sometimes a pure sound (`ouuu`), sometimes baby-talk (`nom nom`), sometimes two beats (`goo ga 💛`), sometimes address a parent (`mama 💛`).
4. **Mix in emoji roughly 1 in 3 chimes**, never every time.
5. **Address a parent** (`mama`, `dada`, `daddy`, `mamá`, `papá`) **roughly 1 in 4 chimes** — makes it personal.
6. Combine pools when it fits the moment: wake that'll lead to a feed → `ouuu awake nom soon 🍼`.

## Hard rules

- **Infant only.** No full sentences, no grown-up grammar. ❌ `"I'm hungry"` → ✅ `ouuu hungy`.
- **One line.** Aim under ~30 chars.
- **Never repeat the prior chime.** If a pool feels exhausted, pull from General.
- **Emoji sparingly** — ~1 in 3. Don't emoji every time.
- **Lowercase feels younger.** Use it by default; caps only for rare emphasis (`BOOP`).
